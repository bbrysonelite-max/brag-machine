# Coaching Style

Teach one thing. The viewer should walk away able to DO something they couldn't 20 seconds ago.

## Creative Laws

1. **18–25 seconds.** Teaching needs a breath more room than a promo.
2. **Hook = the pain, named bluntly** in the first 2–3s ("Your follow-up dies on day 3. Here's why.").
3. **Pattern:** pain → the shift (the ONE insight, accent-colored) → 2–3 concrete steps on cards → do-this-today outro.
4. **Steps are numbered and short** — max 6 words each, snapped in on the beat, each held ≥1s.
5. **One lesson per video.** If it needs two, it's two videos.
6. **No guru-speak.** Banned: "unlock", "level up", "game-changer", "secret". Explain it so a civilian gets it.
7. **Readable:** fast-in, then HOLD. Sentences ~0.3s/word settled time.
8. **Beat-sync:** the shift lands on a strong cue; steps snap to the grid.

## Voice

Brent coaching a rep he actually wants to win: direct, warm underneath, zero fluff. Speaks from doing, not theory. "Here's what actually works" energy.
