# Never-Say Guardrails (CONFIRMED with Brent, 2026-06-17)

Every output must pass this before it can be shown. When in doubt, leave it out.

## Voice / tone — never
- No tech-bro voice. No guru-selling-a-lifestyle. No hype machine. Not corporate.
- No corporate-speak: "leverage synergies", "value proposition", "utilize".
- No fake energy / exclamation-point padding.
- No "folks", "friend", "my friend".

## Claims — never (compliance: FTC + company social-media policy)
- ❌ Income / earnings claims — no dollar figures, no "replace your income", no "passive
  income", no implied or typical earnings.
- ❌ Guarantees of results, rank, income, or success ("you will", "guaranteed").
- ❌ "Get rich" / financial-freedom framing.
- ❌ Health / product efficacy claims of any kind.
- ❌ Naming a specific company (e.g. NuSkin) in any way that implies official endorsement
  or violates its social-media policy.
- ❌ Anything that can't be backed by proof ("value must exceed cost").
- ❌ Disparaging competitors or other companies.

## Brent's track record — biography, NOT earnings
- ALLOWED as biography / credibility: "I built nine Subways in 18 months", "I hit Blue
  Diamond in 12 months", the three windows, 39 years in distribution.
- NEVER framed as what a reader can expect to earn. Past fact about Brent — not a promise.

## Focus rule (audience filter)
- Keep the focus OUTSIDE Brent. Speak to the reader's TEAM, not to Brent's story.
- Use Brent's biography only as brief credibility, then return to "your team".
